3_6_2009 Forgiveness 

Forgiveness is the answer to the child's dream 
of a miracle by which what is broken is made whole again,
what is soiled is again made clean.
-----------Magdalene Chan

Days ago, when my grandma was about to die. She bitterly cursed her daughter-in-laws for treating her badly. What I did then is holding her hands and saying forgiveness. Cause I belive the world would lose its hope without this noble deed.

During the past two weeks, I saw the dark side of human beings myself. I having been fighting with mad dogs for too long. And I am tired. Will I forgive Heqing? Will I be noble myself?

I will not bring somebody else pain because I am suffering, I will try to love those who hate me, I will say love and care when I leave this world. And this is my oath!
