>Standards Drawing PCB

These following standards were used and they worked well

  1. most signal wire is 6mil, a few of them which is very long is changed to 10mil

  2. main VDD wire is 30mil, the branch is 20mil,some very short one in crowed places is 10mil

  3. via(50,28) for 30mil wire,
    via(20,12) for 20mil wire,
    via(40,20) for 20mil wire.

  4. most vertical lines is in the bottom, and horizental the top. This way the wires are clean and tidy, very easy to connect. Whereas, many more via is needed, is that ok?

  5. all the GND pins in crowed places are ought to be funed out,
    because sometimes the polygon fails to reach them.
